About

Castleblayney is a small town situated in the east of County Monaghan, bordering with County Armagh in Northern Ireland and is situated on the N2 motorway from Dublin to Derry.
The town has a population of over 3,500 people and is situated on the western shore of Lough Muckno, County Monaghan's largest lake.
