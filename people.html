People of Interest

Although Castleblayney is considered a small town, it is known to be the home of various peers throughout history and public acknowledgement.

Andrew Thomas Blayney - 1770-1834
Anglo-Irish peer.
Eoin O'Duffy - 1892-1944
General in the Irish Civil War
Second Garda Commissioner
Leader of Fine Gael
Clare Sheridan - 1885-1970
Sculptor
James McMahon Graham - 1852-1945
US emmigrant, turned attorney and United States Represenative for Illinois
Tom 'Big Tom' McBride - 1936-2018
Irish Country singer
Lead Member of Irish showband The Mainliners
